About the Fellowship

HOWLADER Rising Stars
Fellowship 2025

The HOWLADER Rising Stars Fellowship 2025 is a new initiative that invites applications exclusively from third- and fourth-year (or equivalent semester) undergraduate students in Bangladesh who aspire to build careers in Neuroscience.

This fellowship offers monthly support of 4,000 BDT for one year to two selected candidates, empowering fellows to focus on academic growth, explore research opportunities, and build a strong foundation for their future careers in neuroscience.

Fellows join a community of ambitious students preparing for admission to leading PhD programs in Neuroscience and related fields worldwide.

In addition to financial support, fellows receive personalised mentorship and training on preparing strong graduate applications, guidance on academic and career planning, and opportunities to contribute to research projects that may lead to publications in reputed journals.

Open to students in:

  • Computer Science
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Materials Science Engineering
  • Genetic Engineering
  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Biochemistry
  • Microbiology
  • Other related biological & biomedical sciences
